Class: Lgnt

node-api.Currency.Lgnt

Represents an amount of LGNT. The smallest possible amount of LGNT is equal to 10 ^ Lgnt.SMALLEST_UNIT_PREFIX.tenExponent. An amount of LGNT has at most Lgnt.DECIMALS (equal to -Lgnt.SMALLEST_UNIT_PREFIX.tenExponent) decimals.

The canonical form of an amount of LGNT is its expression as a factor of 10 ^ Lgnt.SMALLEST_UNIT_PREFIX.tenExponent. For example, the canonical form of 1 LGNT is equal to 10 ^ Lgnt.DECIMALS.
